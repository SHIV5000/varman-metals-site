FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Difference between SHG and High Grade Zinc? +
SHG (Special High Grade) Zinc has 99.995% purity, while High Grade Zinc has 99.99% purity. SHG is used for premium applications requiring maximum purity, while High Grade is standard for most industrial applications.
What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)? +
Minimum Order Quantity is 1000 Kg (1 Metric Ton) for all zinc ingot grades. For trial orders, samples are available for quality verification.
Do you provide Lab Test Reports? +
Yes, every batch is tested via spectrochemical analysis. Verified lab reports proving 99.9%+ purity are provided with every dispatch for High Grade, SHG, and Pure Zinc ingots.
Do you supply to Meerut and Western UP? +
Yes, we are based in Meerut and extensively supply to industrial areas in Meerut, Moradabad, Bulandshehar, Aligarh, Secunderabad, and surrounding regions in Uttar Pradesh.
