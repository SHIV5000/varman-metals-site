Zinc Price Trends Firm Up for the Diwali Season

Published on: October 18, 2025 | By: Varman Metals

The Diwali season and Dhanteras festival significantly amplify the momentum in the zinc metal market, leading to a firming of Zinc Ingot prices. This bullish trend is fueled by multiple factors.

First, there is a customary pre-festival surge in construction and infrastructure development, which boosts demand for galvanized steel products. Crucially, the tradition of buying metal items for Dhanteras raises massive demand for brass and its products, particularly utensils. Since zinc ingots are an essential component used extensively in the production of brass, this direct festival-related buying creates a tight supply situation for premium alloys like Kaskut Zinc during the peak weeks. We advise clients to secure their bulk supply now to mitigate potential price volatility.
