Uses of Zinc Ingots in India and Import-Export Outlook 2025‚Äì26 üìà

In India, zinc ingots (pure zinc metal slabs) are mainly used in the metal manufacturing industry, especially for galvanizing steel to prevent corrosion. They are also used in die-casting, alloy production (such as brass), and in industries like rubber, aluminium, battery, and chemicals.

üìä 2024‚Äì25 Import-Export Data:

During FY 2024‚Äì25, India imported about 206,783 tonnes of zinc ingots, around 5.2% lower than the previous year. In the same period, approximately 2,085 export shipments were recorded, with Malaysia, Nepal, and Indonesia being the major destinations.

Future Outlook (2025‚Äì26): It is expected that in FY 2025‚Äì26, imports may decline further due to increased domestic production, while exports are likely to continue rising.
